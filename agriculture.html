Agriculture Exams

Start Your Agriculture Career with ICAR AIEEA

ICAR AIEEA, conducted by NTA, is the gateway for undergraduate admissions to Agriculture and Science courses
through ICAR, with exams like CUET and AGRICET also playing a crucial role in shaping your future in agriculture.

All India Exam for Agriculture (ICAR AIEEA)

ICAR AIEEA – Agriculture Entrance Exam

Conducted by:
NTA on behalf of ICAR

Eligibility:
10+2 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
with minimum 50% aggregate (varies)

Seats:
15% UG, 25% PG & PhD at Agricultural Universities

Application Fee:
UG: ₹1,000 (General) / ₹900 (OBC/EWS)
₹800 (SC/ST/PwD) / ₹4,500 (Abroad)

Exam Month:
June

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Emerging Students:
2022 UG: 61,051 appeared
2024 PG: 41,148 appeared

Explore Engineering, Agriculture & Pharmacy with TS EAMCET

TS EAMCET is a state-level exam for admission to B.Tech, Agriculture, and Pharmacy programs
in Telangana, conducted annually by JNTU Hyderabad on behalf of the Telangana State Council.

TS EAMCET – Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test

TS EAMCET – Engineering, Agriculture & Medical Common Entrance

Conducted by:
JNTU Hyderabad on behalf of TSCHE

Eligibility:
10+2 with minimum 45% (40% for reserved categories)

Seats:
85,756 engineering seats + agriculture programs

Application Fee:
₹900 (General), ₹500 (SC/ST/PH)

Exam Month:
April & May (Annual)

Exam Mode:
Online Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
3,20,587 registered
(2,05,295 Engineering | 1,15,292 Agri/Medical)

Pursue UG Programs in Andhra Pradesh with AP EAPCET

AP EAPCET is the state-level entrance exam for engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy
programs in Andhra Pradesh, conducted by JNTU Kakinada for APSCHE.

AP EAMCET – Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test

AP EAMCET – Engineering, Agriculture & Pharmacy Entrance

Conducted by:
JNTU Kakinada on behalf of APSCHE

Eligibility:
10+2 passed or appearing (AP/TS Board or equivalent)

Seats:
1,43,254 engineering seats (1,38,972 private colleges)

Application Fee:
₹600 (General), ₹550 (BC), ₹500 (SC/ST)

Exam Month:
May (Once a year)

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
2,24,724 appeared, 1,71,514 qualified (Engineering)
