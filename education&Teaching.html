Shape the Future with CTET – Education & Teaching

The Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET) is a national-level exam conducted by CBSE for teaching roles in Central Government schools.
CTET certification is essential for educators and widely recognized by private institutions.

Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET)

CTET Details Overview

Conducted by:
Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE)

Eligibility:
12th with 45% marks
Graduation with 50% marks
Post-graduation with 55% marks

Seats:
Approx. 50,000 to 1,00,000 annually

Application Fee:
Gen/OBC: ₹1,000 (one paper), ₹1,200 (both)
SC/ST/PWD: ₹500 (one paper), ₹600 (both)

Exam Month:
July and December (Twice a year)

Exam Mode:
Offline (Pen-and-paper based)

Appearing Candidates:
Registered: 6,86,197
Appeared: 5,72,489
Qualified: 1,38,389

Empower Young Minds with State TET – Education & Teaching

The Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) is a state-level exam ensuring teachers meet standards for government schools.
Conducted by state authorities, it qualifies candidates to educate the next generation.

Teacher Eligibility Test (TET – State-wise)

TET Details Overview

Conducted by:
Respective state governments or their designated bodies

Eligibility:
12th or Graduation + relevant teacher training qualification

Seats:
Varies by state and year

Application Fee:
Tamil Nadu (TNTET): ₹500 (Gen), ₹250 (SC/ST/PWD)
Telangana (TSTET): ₹1,000
Maharashtra (MAHA TET): ₹1,000 (Gen)

Exam Month:
Varies by state

Exam Mode:
Typically Offline (Pen-and-paper based)

Appearing Candidates:
Tamil Nadu TET (2019):
Paper I: 1,62,316 appeared
Paper II: 3,79,735 appeared

Advance in Teaching with CUET PG (Education) – M.Ed. Programs

CUET PG (Education) is a national entrance exam by NTA for M.Ed. programs across India’s universities.
It streamlines admissions to postgraduate education courses, shaping future educators.

CUET PG (Education) – M.Ed. Programs

CUET PG (Education) Details Overview

Conducted by:
National Testing Agency (NTA)

Eligibility:
B.Ed., D.Ed., B.Sc.-B.Ed., or B.A.-B.Ed. with minimum 50% marks

Seats:
Approx. 2,000–3,000 seats across universities

Application Fee:
General: ₹1,200
OBC-NCL/EWS: ₹1,000
SC/ST/Third Gender: ₹900
International: ₹6,000

Exam Month:
March and April

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT) with MCQs

Appearing Candidates:
Approx. 15,000–20,000 annually

Shape the Future with RIE CEE – Education & Teaching

RIE CEE, conducted by NCERT, is a national entrance exam for B.Ed. and M.Ed. programs at RIEs.
It equips future educators with essential pedagogical skills for teaching excellence.

RIE CEE – Regional Institute of Education Common Entrance Exam

RIE CEE Details Overview

Conducted by:
National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT)

Eligibility:
Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% marks (varies by program)

Seats:
Approx. 1,500–2,000 seats across RIE campuses

Application Fee:
General/OBC: ₹900
SC/ST/PH: ₹450

Exam Month:
June (Once a year)

Exam Mode:
Offline (Pen-and-Paper Based)

Appearing Candidates:
Approx. 19,547 candidates
