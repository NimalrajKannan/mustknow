Advance in Teaching with CUET (Education) – M.Ed. Programs

The Common University Entrance Test for Postgraduate programs (CUET) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admissions to postgraduate programs across various universities in India. It's a unified platform that enables multiple universities to select candidates for their M.Ed. programs, streamlining the path to higher education in teaching and education sciences.

CUET (Education) – M.Ed. Programs

CUET (Education) – M.Ed. Programs

Conducted by:
National Testing Agency (NTA)

Eligibility:
B.Ed., D.Ed., B.Sc.-B.Ed., or B.A.-B.Ed.
Minimum 50% marks

Seats:
Approx. 2,000–3,000 seats

Application Fee:
General: ₹1200
OBC-NCL/EWS: ₹1000
SC/ST/Third Gender: ₹900
International: ₹6000

Exam Month:
March and April

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT) with MCQs

Appearing Candidates:
Around 15,000–20,000 annually

Unlock Your Future with SUAT

SUAT (Sharda University Admission Test) is a university-level entrance exam conducted annually by Sharda University for admission into a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses. It covers fields like Engineering, Management, Commerce, Law, and more, serving as a single gateway to diverse academic programs at the university.

SUAT – Sharda University Admission Test

SUAT Details Overview

Conducted by:
Sharda University

Eligibility:
Passed 10+2 with 60-70%
Min. age: 17 years

Seats:
130+ UG, PG, and other programs

Application Fee:
Male: ₹1,500
Female: ₹1,000

Exam Month:
June (Once a year)

Exam Mode & Candidates:
Computer-Based Test (MCQs)
~100,000–150,000 annually

Join the GITAM Community with GSAT

The GITAM Science Admission Test (GSAT) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM) for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs across its campuses in Vishakapatnam, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, and GIMSR. The test is computer-based and offers a pathway to diverse academic fields, including Engineering, Architecture, Humanities, and more.

GSAT – GITAM Science Admission Test

GSAT Details Overview

Conducted by:

Eligibility:
UG: 10+2 with PCM, 60%
PG: Bachelor's with 50% aggregate

Seats:
~5,000–6,000 across programs

Application Fee:
₹1,200

Exam Months:
March & April (Multiple times)

Exam Mode & Candidates:
Computer-Based Test
~55,000–60,000 annually

Prepare for Success with FLAME University Test

The FLAME Entrance Aptitude Test (FEAT) is an online aptitude test for UG programs at FLAME University, designed mainly for students who do not have SAT or ACT scores. This 120-minute exam is divided into four sections: Verbal Ability, Reasoning, Quantitative Ability, and General Knowledge. Applicants who submit SAT/ACT scores may only be required to complete an essay instead of the FEAT.

FLAME University Test – FLAME University Entrance Exam

Prepare for Success with NAT – National Aptitude Test

The National Aptitude Test (NAT) is a national-level entrance exam administered by the Asia Pacific Institute of Information Technology (APIIT). This exam is conducted for admission to undergraduate (B Tech/BBA) and postgraduate (MBA) programs.

NAT – National Aptitude Test

NAT Details Overview

Eligibility:
UG B.Tech/BBA: Minimum 45% in 12th/intermediate exam from a recognized board

Seats:
50% of B.Tech seats (incl. 15% All India Category) through Haryana centralized counseling (JEE Main scores)

Application Fee:
₹700

Exam Month:
June

Location:
APIIT, Panipat
