Pursue Your Legal Career with CLAT

CLAT is a national-level entrance exam for admission to UG and PG law programs at 24 prestigious National Law Universities (NLUs) in India,
conducted by the Consortium of National Law Universities (CNLU).

CLAT – Common Law Admission Test

CLAT – Common Law Admission Test

Conducted by:
Consortium of National Law Universities (CNLU)

Eligibility:
Completed Class 12
General: 45% marks
SC/ST: 40% marks

Seats:
3,213 UG seats (5-year program)
1,217 PG seats (1-year program)

Application Fee:
Gen/OBC/EWS: ₹4,000–₹4,500
SC/ST/PWD: ₹3,500–₹4,000

Exam Month:
December

Exam Mode:
Online Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
75,000+ registered
96.36% attendance (~72,270 appeared)

Embark on Your Legal Career with AILET

AILET is a national-level entrance exam conducted by National Law University, Delhi (NLU Delhi),
for admission to undergraduate (B.A. LL.B. (Hons.)), postgraduate (LL.M.), and PhD law programs.

AILET – All India Law Entrance Test

AILET – All India Law Entrance Test

Conducted by:
National Law University Delhi (NLU Delhi)

Eligibility:
LL.B. or equivalent with 50% marks
(45% for SC/ST/PwD)

Seats:
110 BA LLB and 50 BCom LLB
70 LLM seats through AILET

Application Fee:
Gen/OBC/EWS/Foreign/KM: ₹3,500
SC/ST/PwD: ₹1,500

Exam Month:
December

Exam Mode:
Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQs)

Appearing Candidates:
19,830 registered
18,717 appeared

Join Leading Law Schools with SLAT

SLAT is a national-level entrance exam conducted by Symbiosis International (Deemed University)
for admission to its four prestigious Symbiosis Law Schools (SLS) in Pune, Noida, Nagpur, and Hyderabad.

SLAT – Symbiosis Law Admission Test

SLAT – Entrance for BA LLB & BBA LLB Programs

Conducted by:
Symbiosis International (Deemed University)

Eligibility:
Class 12 pass
45% (General), 40% (SC/ST)

Seats:
1080 seats across 4 campuses

Application Fee:
₹2,250 per test
+ ₹1,000 per SLS program

Exam Month:
December (Once a year)

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
Around 19,000 (for 2025)
