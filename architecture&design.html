Architecture & Design Exams

Build Your Future in Architecture with NATA

NATA is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the Council of Architecture (COA)
for admission to 5-year B.Arch programs at universities and colleges across India.

NATA – National Aptitude Test in Architecture

NATA Details Overview

Conducted by:
Council of Architecture (COA)

Eligibility:
10+2 with Math or 10+3 Diploma (50% min)

Seats:
~19,788 seats in 365 colleges (1 international)

Application Fee:
Gen/OBC Male ₹1,750 | Female ₹1,250
SC/ST/EWS/PWD Male ₹1,250 | Female ₹1,000
Transgender ₹1,000

Exam Months:
March to June (3 attempts/year)

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
50,000 – 60,000 annually

Start Your Design Career with UCEED

UCEED is a national-level entrance exam conducted by IIT Bombay for admission to B.Des programs
at top institutes like IIT Bombay, Delhi, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Roorkee, and IIITDM Jabalpur.

UCEED – Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design

UCEED – National Level Entrance for B.Des

Conducted by:
IIT Bombay

Eligibility:
Passed Class XII (2024 or 2025), all streams eligible

Seats:
245 seats across IITs and IIITDM Jabalpur

Application Fee:
Female/SC/ST/PWD: ₹2,000
Others: ₹4,000

Exam Month:
January (Once a year)

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
15,408 appeared, 5,703 qualified

Pursue Postgraduate Design Programs with CEED

CEED is a national-level entrance exam conducted by IIT Bombay for admission to M.Des programs
at IITs and other leading design institutes across India.

CEED – Common Entrance Examination for Design

CEED – National Level Entrance for M.Des Programs

Conducted by:
IIT Bombay on behalf of Ministry of Education (MoE)

Eligibility:
Minimum 55% (50% for SC/ST/PwD) or equivalent grades in qualifying degree

Seats:
Around 300 M.Des seats across IITs and top design institutes

Application Fee:
Female/SC/ST/PwD: ₹2,000
Others: ₹4,000

Exam Month:
January (Once a year)

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
Around 5,000 appeared; 1,878 qualified

Unlock Your Creativity with the NID Entrance Exam

The NID Design Aptitude Test (DAT) is a two-stage national-level exam for admission to design programs
at National Institute of Design campuses—featuring a written test, studio test, and interview.

NID Entrance Exam – Design Aptitude Test (DAT)

NID DAT – National Level Exam for B.Des & M.Des

Conducted by:
National Institute of Design

Eligibility:
Completed 10+2 in any stream from a recognized board (for B.Des)

Seats:
B.Des – 425 seats across 5 campuses
M.Des – 347 seats across 3 campuses

Application Fee:
General/OBC/EWS: ₹3,000
SC/ST/PwD: ₹1,500

Exam Month:
Prelims – January
M.Des Mains – March to April
B.Des Mains – April to May

Exam Pattern:
Two stages – DAT Prelims (written) and DAT Mains (studio test + interview)

Appearing Candidates:
Around 15,000 students appeared for Prelims

Begin Your Design Journey with SEED

SEED is the entrance exam for admission to undergraduate design programs at Symbiosis Institute of Design (SID),
selecting creative and skilled candidates with a passion for design.

SEED – Symbiosis Entrance Exam for Design

SEED – Entrance Exam for Design Programs

Conducted by:
Symbiosis Institute of Design (SID)

Eligibility:
Passed 10+2 (any stream) with 50% (45% for SC/ST)
OR 10+3 diploma from State Board

Seats:
207 B.Des seats (180 Indian, 27 International)

Application Fee:
₹3,250 (₹2,250 SEED + ₹1,000 SID)

Exam Month:
January

Exam Mode:
Online proctored (PC/Laptop-based)

Appearing Candidates:
Approximately 50,000 students annually
