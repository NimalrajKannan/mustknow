Explore Engineering & Technology with JEE

JEE Main, conducted by NTA, is India’s key exam for admission into IITs, NITs & CFTIs.
It also qualifies students for JEE Advanced to enter top engineering institutes.

JEE – Joint Entrance Examination for Engineering

JEE – Joint Entrance Examination (Main & Advanced)

Conducted by:
National Testing Agency (NTA)

Eligibility:
Passed/Appearing Class 12
PCM Subjects

Seats:
~42,177 Seats (NITs, IIITs, GFTIs)

Application Fee:
Male: ₹1,000 | Female: ₹800
SC/ST/PwD: ₹500

Exam Months:
January & April

Appearing Students:
~13.8–15 lakh annually
