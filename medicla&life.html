Discover NEET – Medical & Life Sciences Career Pathways

NEET (UG) is India’s national entrance exam for MBBS & medical courses, conducted by NTA.
NEET PG offers postgraduate opportunities in medical education across India.

NEET – National Eligibility cum Entrance Exam for Medicine

NEET Details Overview

Conducted by:
National Testing Agency (NTA)

Eligibility:
Gen: 50% in PCB
OBC/SC/ST: 40%
PWD: 45%

Seats:
109,170 MBBS seats
41,388 in Govt Colleges

Application Fee:
Gen: ₹1,700 | OBC: ₹1,600 | Reserved: ₹1,000

Exam Month:
May (Once a year)

Success Rate:
~56% (23 lakh applicants)

Explore Medical PG Courses with INI-CET

INI-CET, conducted by AIIMS, is the gateway to MD, MS, MDS & DM/MCh admissions in top
Institutes of National Importance like AIIMS, JIPMER, PGIMER, NIMHANS & SCTIMST.

INI-CET – Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test
INI-CET – National-Level PG Medical Entrance

into doctoral programs across various disciplines at AIIMS.

Begin Your Research Journey with ICMR JRF

The ICMR JRF exam is a national-level test conducted by ICMR to determine eligibility
for Junior Research Fellowships for Indian National candidates.

ICMR JRF – Indian Council of Medical Research – Junior Research Fellowship

ICMR JRF – Junior Research Fellowship

Conducted by:
Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR)

Eligibility:
M.Sc./M.A. in relevant science
55% (Gen/OBC/EWS), 50% (SC/ST/PH/VH)

Seats:
150 JRFs + 100 Project Positions

Application Fee:
General/OBC/EWS: ₹1,500
SC/ST: ₹1,200

Exam Month:
July (Once a year)

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
290 Life Sciences, 9 Social Sciences (recent)
