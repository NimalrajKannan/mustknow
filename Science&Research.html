Pursue Dual Degree in Science with IISER IAT

The IISER Aptitude Test (IAT) is the exclusive entrance exam for the 5-year BS-MS
dual degree program at the Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research (IISERs).

IISER IAT – Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research Aptitude Test

IISER IAT – BS-MS, BS, and BTech Programs

Conducted by:
IISERs

Eligibility:
Class 12 with Biology, Chemistry, Maths, or Physics
Gen/OBC/EWS: 60% aggregate
SC/ST/PwD: 55% aggregate

Seats:
2,363 across 7 IISERs

Application Fee:
Gen/EWS/OBC: ₹2,000
SC/ST/PwD/KM: ₹1,000

Exam Month:
May

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
~2 Lakh apply, 40% clear

Join Top Science Institutes via NEST

The National Entrance Screening Test (NEST) is the gateway to the 5-year Integrated MSc
program at NISER Bhubaneswar and UM–DAE CEBS, Mumbai in Biology, Chemistry, Math & Physics.

NEST – National Entrance Screening Test

NEST – 5-Year Integrated MSc Programs

Conducted by:
NISER & UM-DAE CEBS

Eligibility:
Class 12 (Science)
Gen/OBC: 60% aggregate
SC/ST/Divyangjan: 55% aggregate
Qualify NEST 2025 merit list

Seats:
257 (200 NISER, 57 CEBS)

Application Fee:
Male/UR/OBC: ₹1,400
Female/SC/ST/Divyangjan: ₹700

Exam Month: June

Exam Mode: Online CBT

Appearing: 25–30K candidates

Shape Your Tech Future at IIIT-H via UGEE/SAT

IIIT Hyderabad’s UGEE/SAT route offers admission to B.Tech programs through a 3-hour
computer-based exam with SUPR (Subject Proficiency) and REAP (Research Aptitude) sections.

UGEE/SAT – IIIT Hyderabad Undergraduate Entrance Exam

UGEE/SAT – Research-Oriented B.Tech Programs

Conducted by:
IIIT Hyderabad

Eligibility:
Class 12 with PCM
Min. 60% in Physics, Chemistry, Maths

Seats:
100–120 admissions

Application Fee:
Male: ₹3,000
Female: ₹1,500

Exam Month:
April

Exam Mode:
CBT: SUPR (60 min), REAP (120 min)
25% negative marking

Appearing Candidates:
30–60K annually

Build a Research & Academic Career via CSIR NET

The CSIR NET, conducted by NTA, qualifies candidates for Junior Research Fellowships (JRF) and
Lectureships/Assistant Professorships in science and technology across Indian universities.

CSIR NET – National Eligibility Test

CSIR NET – JRF and Lectureship

Conducted by:
NTA

Eligibility:
M.Sc. or equiv.
Gen/OBC: 55% marks
SC/ST/PwD: 50% marks

Seats:
~600–700 Lectureships

Application Fee:
Gen: ₹1,150
OBC-NCL: ₹600
SC/ST/PwD: ₹325

Exam Months:
June, December

Exam Mode:
CBT

Appearing:
~1.6L appeared
