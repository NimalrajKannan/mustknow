Step Into the World of Cinema with FTII JET

The FTII JET is a national-level entrance exam conducted by FTII Pune or SRFTI Kolkata. It offers admission to PG diploma & PG courses in film and television disciplines, launching careers in the cinematic arts.

Unlock Your Creative Future with SRFTI JET

The SRFTI - Satyajit Ray Film and Television Institute Entrance Exam is a Joint Entrance Test (JET) conducted by FTII, Pune, and SRFTI, Kolkata for admissions to postgraduate and post-graduate diploma courses in film and television.
It is a prestigious exam that opens doors to a career in the dynamic world of cinema.

Explore Your Creative Potential with WWI Entrance

The Whistling Woods International (WWI) Entrance Exam is a competitive test for aspiring students in Film, Communication, and Creative Arts.
The exam consists of two parts: a General Aptitude Test (GAT) focusing on logical reasoning and analytical abilities, and a Creative Ability Test (CAT) assessing creativity and design aptitude.
It is conducted program-wise with specific dates for different courses like B.Des in Fashion Design, Animation, Game Design, and Visual Communication Design.

Step into the World of Drama with NSD Screening Test

The NSD entrance exam is a two-stage process for admission to the Diploma in Dramatic Arts course at the prestigious National School of Drama in Delhi.
The process includes a preliminary examination followed by a final workshop, designed to assess the skills and potential of aspiring students in the field of dramatic arts.
