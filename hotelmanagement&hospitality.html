Step into Hospitality with NCHMCT JEE

NCHMCT JEE is a national-level entrance exam conducted by NTA
for admission to B.Sc. in Hospitality & Hotel Administration at top IHMs across India.

NCHMCT JEE – National Council for Hotel Management and Catering Technology Joint Entrance Examination

NCHMCT JEE – Entrance for B.Sc. Hospitality & Hotel Administration

Conducted by:
National Testing Agency (NTA)

Eligibility:
Class 12 pass with English compulsory

Seats:
Approx. 11,965 across 75 IHMs

Application Fee:
General/OBC: ₹1,000
EWS: ₹700
SC/ST/PwD: ₹450

Exam Month:
May (Once a year)

Exam Mode:
Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Appearing Candidates:
12,738 registered, 8,862 appeared (2024)

Begin Your Hospitality Career with IIHM e-CHAT

IIHM e-CHAT is a national-level online exam for admission to UG, diploma,
and certificate courses in hospitality and hotel management at IIHM campuses.

IIHM e-CHAT – International Institute of Hotel Management – Electronic Common Hospitality Admission Test

IIHM e-CHAT – Entrance for Hospitality & Hotel Management Programs

Conducted by:
IIHM

Eligibility:
10+2 with English
Under 22 years old

Seats:
Approx. 500–700 across campuses

Application Fee:
₹600
